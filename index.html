BROTHER (DELUXE)

Brother (Deluxe) by Iman is a record he uses to recollect the good old days he spent with his brothers in the hood. He really came through on this catalogue, showing his talent. However, its production was handled by Iman alongside Z3na and Cypress Hits.

BIOGRAPHY

Buwa Tonwe Churchill, better known as Iman is a Nigerian singer-songwriter, born on 14th September 1996 who hails from Jakpa, Warri North local government Area, Delta State. Iman is a BSc holder in geology and petroleum studies and MSc holder of hydrogeology who graduated from Western Delta University (W.D.U).
